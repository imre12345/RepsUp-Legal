RepsUp is a fitness application for iOS and watchOS designed for workout planning, tracking, and analysis. Users can create and customize detailed workout plans by defining exercises, sets, repetitions, rest times, and weight information. These plans can be organized in either a calendar schedule or an ordered list. During a workout, the app guides the user through their session. When used with a paired Apple Watch and with the user's explicit consent, the app can integrate with Apple's HealthKit to track real-time health metrics, including heart rate and active calories burned. After completing a session, the app stores a detailed log of the workout, allowing users to review their history, track aggregate statistics, and analyze their performance over time.
