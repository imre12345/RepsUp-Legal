1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: The Service processes personal information that you provide and create, which is stored locally on your device.

The Service processes personal information that you voluntarily provide when you create workout plans, track your fitness activities, or otherwise use the features of the Services. We, the developer, do not collect this information or have any access to it.

Personal Information Provided by You. The personal information that the Service processes depends on the context of your interactions with the Services and the features you use. This information is created by you and stored on your device. The personal information processed may include the following:

  • Workout and Exercise Data: This includes workout names, exercise names, and parameters you define such as sets, repetitions (reps), rest times, and weight data (including the unit, e.g., kg or lbs).
  • Personal Notes: Optional notes that you can add to specific exercises.

Sensitive Information

In Short: With your explicit consent, the Service can access specific health data from Apple's HealthKit framework on your device to provide workout-related features. This data is never transmitted to us.

With your explicit consent, the Service processes the following categories of sensitive information by accessing them from Apple's HealthKit framework on your device:

  • Health Data: We request permission to read heart rate and active energy burned (calories) during a workout session. Heart rate is displayed live on your Apple Watch for real-time feedback. Active energy data is used solely for the purpose of saving your completed workout to the Apple Health app, which contributes to your Activity Rings. This calorie data is not displayed within the RepsUp application itself. This data is stored on your device as part of your completed workout history and is never transmitted to us.

Application Data

In Short: If you use our application, it may request access to certain device features to enable its functionality. All related data and processing occur on your device.

If you use our application(s), it may request access or permission to certain features from your mobile device to enable core functionality. All processing occurs locally on your device.

  • Mobile Device Access. The Service may request access or permission to certain features from your mobile device, including your mobile device's Health Data (via HealthKit). This access is required to read heart rate and calorie data during a workout and to save the completed workout to your Apple Health database. If you wish to change our access or permissions, you may do so in your device's Settings app.
  • Push Notifications. The Service may request permission to send you local notifications on your device. These are used solely for functional purposes, such as alerting you when a rest timer is complete. We do not send marketing or promotional notifications. If you wish to opt out from receiving these types of communications, you may turn them off in your device's settings.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: The application processes your information locally on your device to provide, improve, and administer the Services. We do not have access to your information.

The application processes your personal information on your device for a variety of reasons, depending on how you interact with our Services, including:

  • To deliver and facilitate the delivery of services to the user. The application processes your information to provide you with the requested service, such as creating and managing workout plans.
  • Workout Planning and Management. To store, organize, and display the workout plans and exercises that you create, including your custom sets, reps, weights, and notes.
  • Workout Session Tracking. To guide you through your planned workout sessions, track your progress, and manage timers for sets and rest periods.
  • Performance History and Analytics. To save a log of your completed workouts and process this history to provide you with aggregate statistics and performance charts, all of which are generated and displayed on your device.
  • Inter-Device Data Synchronization. To securely synchronize your workout plans and completed session data between your paired iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's frameworks.
  • To provide timely workout alerts. To send local notifications on your device to alert you when a rest timer is complete.

4. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

In Short: We do not have access to your personal information and therefore do not share it with any third parties. You may choose to share your data with Apple's Health app.

We do not have access to your personal information and therefore do not share it with any third parties. The architecture of the Service is designed to make such sharing technically impossible from our end.

User-Initiated Sharing with Apple Health

With your explicit consent, you can instruct the application to save your completed workout data to your personal Apple Health database on your device. This may include data such as workout type, duration, and total active energy burned. The Service synchronizes your workout data between your paired iPhone and Apple Watch. This transfer occurs using Apple's secure WatchConnectivity framework and the data remains within your personal ecosystem of devices.

Business Transfers

If we sell our business, assets, or merge with another company, the transaction will not include any of your personal information. This is because all user data is stored exclusively on users' personal devices and is not accessible to us or stored in any company-controlled database.

8. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?

In Short: Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have direct and complete control over it. You can exercise your rights at any time without needing to contact us.

In some regions (like the EEA, UK, Switzerland, and Canada), you have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. These may include the right (i) to request access and obtain a copy of your personal information, (ii) to request rectification or erasure; (iii) to restrict the processing of your personal information; and (iv) if applicable, to data portability.

Because RepsUp stores all your personal information directly and exclusively on your device, you can exercise these rights directly, giving you complete control over your data.

  • Right to Access, Review, and Update: You can access, review, and update your workout plans and history directly within the application's interface at any time.
  • Right to Delete (Erasure): You can delete your personal information at any time by deleting the RepsUp application from your device(s). Deleting the application permanently removes all associated data from that device.
  • Right to Control Consent (HealthKit): You can manage or withdraw your consent for HealthKit access at any time through the Apple Health app or your device's main Settings app under "Health" > "Data Access & Devices" > "RepsUp."
  • Inapplicability of Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs): Please note that because we do not collect, store, or have any access to your personal information, traditional data subject access requests (e.g., requesting a copy of your data from us) are not applicable. All of your data resides on your device, under your control.
  • Right to Data Portability and Export: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. The application provides a feature in the Settings screen that allows you to export a complete copy of all your workout and health data as a JSON file, which you can then keep for your records or use with other services.

If you have questions or comments about your privacy rights, you may email us at repsup.privacy@gmail.com.

12. CORE ARCHITECTURAL PRINCIPLE: ON-DEVICE DATA STORAGE

RepsUp is fundamentally designed with a 'privacy-first' architecture. A core principle of the Service is that it operates without any backend servers controlled by the Developer. All personal information you create or that is generated through your use of the App, including your workout plans, session history, and any sensitive health data accessed via Apple's HealthKit, is stored exclusively and securely on your personal Apple device(s) (i.e., your iPhone and/or Apple Watch). The Developer has no technical means to access, view, or process your personal information. This on-device storage model is an intentional design choice to ensure that you, the user, remain in complete control of your data at all times, thereby eliminating risks associated with server-side data breaches and external data transfers.
